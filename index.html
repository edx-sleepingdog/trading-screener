Trading System Rules

📊 LONG Entry Setup (All Must Be True)

  • Price pulls back to or pushes through 20 SMA
  • Stock must be in uptrend (20 > 50 > 200 SMAs)
  • Price within 20% of 200 SMA (not overextended)
  • Relative strength vs SPY positive (outperforming)
  • Sector showing momentum (accelerating strength)
  • Volume confirmation: Pullback on below-average volume, entry day on above-average volume
  • No earnings within 7 days

📉 SHORT Entry Setup (All Must Be True)

  • Price below 200 SMA
  • Downtrend (20 < 50 < 200 SMAs)
  • Failed rally to 20 SMA
  • Relative weakness vs SPY
  • Volume confirmation on down days
  • No earnings within 7 days

⚠️ SHORT WARNING: Unlimited risk - be extra strict with 1% rule.

💰 Position Sizing

  • Risk 1% of account per trade (conservative)
  • Stop loss = 2x ATR(20) from entry
  • Position size = (Account × Risk%) ÷ (Entry - Stop)

Exit Management

  • Hold to +2R target
  • At +2R: Move stop to breakeven
  • After +2R: Trail stop at 20 SMA ± 1 ATR

🛑 Risk Rules

  • Maximum 5 concurrent positions
  • No more than 2 positions in same sector
  • Maximum 2 short positions at any time
  • If 3 consecutive losses, reduce size by 50%
